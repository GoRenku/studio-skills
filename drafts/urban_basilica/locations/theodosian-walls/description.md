## Description

The ancient land walls of Constantinople, scarred by bombardment, patched nightly with timber, barrels, rubble, and prayer.

## Visual Notes

- Dust drifting from cracked stone
- Smoke lying low in the moat
- Tiny human figures against impossible masonry
